== Kernel ==
- Appiled Android O DP2 patches
- f2fs: Add missed 'bio_put(bio)' in 'f2fs_submit_discard_endio()'
- nanohub: Applied lastest marlin patches and Android O DP2 patch from Google msm
- kernel: Only expose su when daemon is running (from LineageOS android_kernel_oneplus_msm8996)
- kernel: Fix potential refcount leak in su check (from LineageOS android_kernel_oneplus_msm8996)
- sdcardfs: Applied lastest android-3.10 patches from Google android-3.10
- net/packet: Fix overflow patches from Google android-3.10
- msm_thermal: Check high temp and decrease LITTLE's frequency to lower values
( When big core is turned off, LITTLE's decrease step will set to 2
by 'temp_count_max_little_with_big_off'.
 In some cases, when device is too hot, there is no way to decrease frequency.
 It needs to check high temp to get lower frequencies of LITTLE cores.)
- arm/arm64: Applies some complie related patches from Google android-3.10

- Updater: Update libraries to O
- Updater: Main: Do not call 'getApplicationContext()' when not needed
- Updater: Fix SELinux denials and check root status
- Updater: Version up to v1.8
- A2DP Checker: Update libraries to O
